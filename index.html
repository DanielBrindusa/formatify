Formatify guide

Convert files online with a fast browser workflow

Formatify helps you convert common files directly in your browser. Use it for image conversion, PDF export, DOCX to PDF or TXT, XLSX to PDF, CSV, or TXT, and quick ICO generation for app or website icons.

Popular conversions

  • PNG to JPG, JPEG, ICO, or PDF
  • JPG and JPEG to PNG, ICO, or PDF
  • PDF to PNG, JPG, JPEG, or TXT
  • DOCX to PDF or TXT
  • XLSX to PDF, CSV, or TXT

Why use Formatify

The app is built for a simpler experience: load a file, preview it, choose the target format, convert it, then download individual results or everything at once when multiple files are generated.

FAQ

Does Formatify upload my files to a server?
Core conversions run in the browser, which helps keep the workflow fast and simple.

Can I convert multiple output files in one run?
Yes. When a conversion creates multiple results, you can download each file individually or use Download All.

What is Formatify best for?
It is ideal for quick everyday conversions, favicon creation, image export, PDF page extraction, and light office document conversions.
